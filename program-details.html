About this Elective

The B.Com with elective in Accounting and Finance is one of the preferred UGC Entitled online degree programs in India. Accounting and finance plays a crucial role in centralizing business operations. Developing a thorough knowledge of finance and accounting is necessary to handle the intricacies of any business effectively. The verticals of accountancy consolidate the techniques to prepare and manage financial hitherto. Financial verticals utilize every available detail to capitalize as per the ballpark. While finance verticals further identify the potential for profit and growth of an organization and accountancy encapsulates every relevant technique to strategize the company's finances.

The unison of these two streams will open a gateway of dynamic career opportunities apart from building one's core skills. The pursuer is at the forefront of various electives; Finance, Accounting, Management, and Economics are the four mainstream parts of the program's curriculum. The seeker gains access to the information about the areas that lay the foundations for commercialism and prepares to excel in every stream including auditing, financial reporting, investment banking, financial analysis, financial advisory and consulting. The online degree programs in India are becoming increasingly popular with the revolutions in the virtual world.
  • Strong foundation in the principles and practices of Accounting and Finance
  • Thorough understanding of advanced courses like Forensic Accounting, FinTech, IFRS, etc.
  • Courses mirror national and global professional certifications and qualifications in Accounting and Finance
  • A blend of accounting, finance and management courses
  • Industry designed curriculum that delivers technical excellence and supplements professional skills
  • A host of skill enhancement and ability enhancement courses in addition to core courses
