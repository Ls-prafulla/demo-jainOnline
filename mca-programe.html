Program Overview

An Online MCA is a two-year master’s degree program perfect for those who wish to pursue a career in the IT domain. Based on AICTE’s model curriculum, our Online MCA Degree Program trains the learners in various aspects of application software, operating systems, computer programs, computer architecture, etc., enhancing their overall technical knowledge. As for working professionals, Online MCA courses allow them to upskill and pursue the degree while working. It prepares them for managerial and corporate roles enabling them to learn trailblazing technological practices and practical exposure to the latest industry requirements, thus making it the best Online MCA course.

Eligibility

Pass in an Undergraduate (Bachelor) Program of a minimum duration of three (3) years in any stream from a UGC Recognized University, with a minimum aggregate of 50% or an equivalent letter/numerical grade. A relaxation of 5% shall be given to SC/ST candidates.

Candidates who are in the final semester of the Bachelor's Program are also eligible to apply.
