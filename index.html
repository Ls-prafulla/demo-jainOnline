Program Overview

An Online MBA is a great choice for those who wish to pursue a career in business management. This Online Degree Program opens up a plethora of career opportunities, increases your earning potential, helps you develop your management skills, and grow your professional network. As for working professionals, an Online MBA program will allow you to gain a valid MBA Degree recognized across the world, without having to take a break from your professional career, and prepare you for a managerial role.

A two-year online degree program that offers foundation courses, and core courses, along with an option to pursue a cross-functional and open elective

  • Based on AICTE's model curriculum as prescribed for a full-time Online MBA Program
  • Designed keeping in mind the industry requirements to provide an in-depth understanding
  • Increases the learner's business knowledge and leadership skills, thus making it the best Online MBA course

Program Highlights
